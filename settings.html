Kick-> Streamerbot | Bridge Settings

How to use Reward Action Prefix

  • The Reward Action Prefix is used to generate Streamer.bot action names for Kick rewards.
  • For example, if the prefix is KickReward- and the reward title is Shoutout, the action name will be KickReward-Shoutout.
  • You can change the prefix to match your own Streamer.bot action naming scheme.

How to use in OBS

  • Fill in your Kick username and Streamer.bot details, then click Save Settings.
  • Click Copy Bridge URL to copy your personalized URL.
  • In OBS, add a new Browser Source and paste the copied URL.
  • The bridge will connect your Kick chat and rewards to Streamer.bot using your settings.
