Kick ➔ Streamer.bot Bridge

Simple Kick Chat + Rewards Bridge

Using Reward Action Prefix

  • The Reward Action Prefix sets your Streamer.bot action names for Kick rewards.
  • Example: If prefix is KickReward- and reward title is Shoutout, action is KickReward-Shoutout.
  • Customize it to match your action names.

Using in OBS

  • Enter your Kick username and Streamer.bot details, then click Save Settings.
  • Copy your personalized URL with Copy Bridge URL.
  • Add a Browser Source in OBS with the copied URL.
  • The bridge will sync your Kick chat and rewards to Streamer.bot.
